# Welcome to Bubble's Brain

Bubble's Brain is a structured AI knowledge guide. It turns daily news, technical articles, and real-world projects into digestible “reading cards” so you can build a solid mental map amid the information flood.

## How to read

- Browse by date in the Daily Brief.
- Subscribe via RSS to keep up with updates.
- Use tags and search to jump across topics and connect ideas.
