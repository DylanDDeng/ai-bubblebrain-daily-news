How It Works

  • 10 colorful balls bounce inside a rotating hexagon with realistic physics.
  • Gravity pulls balls downward (relative to screen).
  • Elasticity determines how much energy is preserved during collisions.
  • Friction gradually slows down the balls' movement.
  • Collision detection handles ball-to-ball and ball-to-hexagon interactions.
  • The hexagon rotates continuously, creating dynamic boundary conditions.
  • Adjust parameters using sliders to see different behaviors!
